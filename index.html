Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026

Welcome to Quack War: Global Clash. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using the app, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide the game experience:

  • Click counts: The number of taps you make, associated with your chosen country — stored anonymously on Firebase.
  • Country selection: The country you choose to represent, stored locally on your device.
  • Device ID: An anonymous identifier used to lock your country selection to your device. No personal information is tied to this ID.
  • Approximate location: Used only at first launch via IP to suggest your country. We do not store or track your location.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not require account registration.
  • We do not track your location beyond the initial country suggestion.
  • We do not sell your data to any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Realtime Database: Used to store and sync global click counts. Data is stored anonymously. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

4. Advertising

Quack War displays ads provided by Google AdMob, including banner ads and rewarded video ads. These ads help keep the game free.

AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

5. Children's Privacy

Quack War is a casual game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal data to function.

6. Data Storage & Security

All game data (click counts) is stored on Google Firebase servers and is protected by Google's security infrastructure. Your personal preferences (country selection, click history) are stored locally on your device only.

7. Your Rights

  • You can reset your game data by clearing the app's storage in your device settings.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored data.
  • Since we do not collect personal information, there is no account to delete.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
